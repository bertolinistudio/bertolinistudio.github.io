About

Founded in 1992 by Tiziano on the shores of Lake Como, the studio welcomed his son Maurizio in 2019, marking a new chapter in its evolution.

Specializing in residential design and public spaces like offices, shops, and restaurants, the studio oversees both new constructions and renovations.

We are inspired by the magnificent architecture of Italian history and its rich artisanal craftsmanship. What drives us is the way every project, big or small, can contribute to improving the status quo. With a thoughtful approach that enhances the existing and unlocks its potential, we strive for a "wise" architecture: lasting, refined, and deeply respectful of its surroundings.
